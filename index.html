About Us

Founded in 2025, AutoVerse India was born from a simple idea — that every Indian deserves world-class automobile services without compromise.

Based in Chandigarh, Punjab, we combine cutting-edge technology with deep automotive expertise to serve individuals and businesses across North India.

From traditional combustion engines to the latest Electric Vehicles, we are ready for the future — and we're taking our customers with us.
